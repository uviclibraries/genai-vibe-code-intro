## Pre-Workshop Videos & Activities
This workshop is primarily hands-on practice with Generative AI tools to "vibe code" web applcations or learning games. To participate fully please do the following **before the workshop**:

- If you're not sure what Vibe Coding is, this video will get you up to speed in 5 minutes: [Vibe Coding - Explained like I am 5](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8p90QcXh5vQ){:target="_blank"} (5 min)<br>
